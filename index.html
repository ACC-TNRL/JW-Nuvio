🎬 JustWatch Nuvio Addon

Private Stremio-compatible catalog addon with JustWatch trending charts for Nuvio. Each manifest below can be added as a separate addon in Nuvio.

📦 Full Manifests (all time periods)

One manifest per country — includes both monthly & weekly charts.

manifest-nl.json
JustWatch Netherlands trending — monthly + weekly
NLMonthlyWeeklyMoviesSeries
manifest-us.json
JustWatch United States trending — monthly + weekly
USMonthlyWeeklyMoviesSeries

📅 Split Manifests (one time period per country)

Add only the time period you want. Monthly = 30-day trending, Weekly = 7-day trending.

manifest-nl-monthly.json
NL — monthly trending only
NLMonthly
manifest-nl-weekly.json
NL — weekly trending only
NLWeekly
manifest-us-monthly.json
US — monthly trending only
USMonthly
manifest-us-weekly.json
US — weekly trending only
USWeekly

🇳🇱 FilmVandaag.nl — Popular in the Netherlands

Dutch popular movies & series from FilmVandaag.nl. Scraped daily, IMDb IDs from detail pages.

manifest-fv.json
FilmVandaag popular movies + series
NLMoviesSeries
